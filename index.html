Decoding Stigma is a new working group affiliated with Hacking//Hustling. We are building a cross-institutional coalition of laborers, futurists, advocates, artists, designers, technologists, researchers, teachers, and students who want to compel conversations about sex and sex work at the tech school.

Our goals are to:
  1. Prioritize sexual autonomy as a necessary ethics question for researchers and technologists
  2. Design a liberatory imaginary in which sexually stigmatized voices are amplified and celebrated.
